Showcase is a lightweight, student-driven portfolio that represents only the work students are proud to share.

Showcase is not a place for teachers to assign a grade, nor decide what is included. Students have complete control over their Showcase portfolio, and never need to include language justifying a low grade or how they plan on improving. With this control, students can share their portfolios at family conferences, with potential employers or even when applying to college.

Students can upload images of physical work and link to any publicly accessibly URL. Coming soon: Google Classroom integration!
